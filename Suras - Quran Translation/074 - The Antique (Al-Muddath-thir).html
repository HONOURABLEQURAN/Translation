In The Name of ALLAH, The Gracious, The Compassionate.
 
74:1 O you Antique!
 
74:2 Get up and warn.
 
74:3 Magnify your OWNER.
 
74:4 And purify your garments.
 
74:5 And move away from wrath.
 
74:6 And do not make grateful by increasing.
 
74:7 And be patient for your OWNER.
 
74:8 Then, when the horn is blown.
 
74:9 That will be a difficult day.
 
74:10 For the disbelievers, not easy.
 
74:11 Leave ME and whom I created alone.
 
74:12 And I granted to him an extensive wealth.
 
74:13 And children to behold.
 
74:14 And I have arranged smoothness for him.
 
74:15 Then he coveted for more.
 
74:16 Never! Indeed, he has been stubborn to OUR verses.
 
74:17 Soon I will get him closer to a climb.
 
74:18 Indeed he thought and decided.
 
74:19 So, may he be slain, how he decided!
 
74:20 Therefore may he be slain, how he decided!
 
74:21 Then he looked.
 
74:22 Then he frowned and scowled.
 
74:23 Then he turned away arrogantly.
 
74:24 So, he said: "This is nothing except transmitted sorcery".
 
74:25 "This is nothing except word of a human".
 
74:26 Soon I will burn him in Saqar.
 
74:27 And do you know what is Saqar?
 
74:28 It neither remains (anything back) nor leaves (alone).
 
74:29 (It shows) tablets to people.
 
74:30 Over it is Nineteen (19).
 
74:31 WE appointed angels to be guardians of Hell, and WE assigned their number (19) to disturb the disbelievers, to convince those who were given the book (that this is a divine scripture), to strengthen the faith of the faithful, to remove all traces of doubt from the hearts of those who were given the book, as well as the believers, and to expose those who harbor doubt in their hearts, and the disbelievers; they will say, "What did ALLAH mean by this allegory?" ALLAH thus sends astray whomever HE wills, and guides whomever HE wills. None knows the soldiers of your LORD except HE. This is a reminder for the people.
 
74:32 Absolutely, (I swear) to the moon.
 
74:33 And the night as it passes.
 
74:34 And the morning as it shines.
 
74:35 Absoluetly, this is one of the greatest ones.
 
74:36 A warner to the human beings.
 
74:37 For those among you who wish to advance, or regress.
 
74:38 Every individual is a hostage for what it earned.
 
74:39 Except the companions of the right.
 
74:40 While in Paradise, they will ask.
 
74:41 About the guilty.
 
74:42 "What inserted you into Saqar?"
 
74:43 They will say, "We did not observe the contact prayers (Salat).
 
74:44 "We did not feed the poor.
 
74:45 "We blundered with the blunderers.
 
74:46 "We disbelieved in the Day of Judgment.
 
74:47 "Until certainty came to us now."
 
74:48 The intercession of the intercessors will never help them.
 
74:49 Why are they so averse to this reminder?
 
74:50 Running like zebras.
 
74:51 Who are fleeing from the lion!
 
74:52 Does each one of them want to receive the scripture personally?
 
74:53 Indeed, they do not fear the Hereafter.
 
74:54 Indeed, this is a reminder.
 
74:55 For those who wish to take heed.
 
74:56 They cannot take heed against ALLAH's will. HE is the source of righteousness; HE is the source of forgiveness.
